FAQ

What is FAETCon?

A conference about electronics, avionics, embedded development, and badge hacking, run by KAI's Faculty of Air Navigation, Electronics and Telecommunications (FAET) — inspired by Hackaday Supercon.

What is "badge hacking"?

Attendees get an in-house electronic badge and can modify, reflash, or solder onto it right at the conference. See the Badge page for details.

Do I need a ticket to submit a talk?

No. You can submit a talk or workshop proposal regardless of a ticket — accepted speakers get free admission.

Can I apply if I've never spoken before?

Yes — we deliberately keep room in the program for first-time speakers. The shorter 20-minute track is a great place to start.

When do ticket sales and the CFP open?

Dates are TBD — follow announcements. The best way to hear first is to follow updates or reach out via Contacts.

Who organizes the event?

The Faculty of Air Navigation, Electronics and Telecommunications (FAET), Kyiv Aviation Institute (KAI).
