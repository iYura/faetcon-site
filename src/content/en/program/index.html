Program

FAETCon's format: two parallel talk tracks, small-group workshops, and a badge-hacking area running all day. Below is a draft plan for the day; the final schedule with speaker names and topics will be published once the Call for Proposals closes.

Draft plan for the day

October 28, 2026 · KAI, Kyiv

BlockWhat happens
Morning — registration Doors open, early-bird registration, and badge pickup.
Opening remarks A welcome from the FAET organizing team.
Daytime — two talk tracks Center Stage — 45-minute talks; Small Stage — 20-minute talks. The tracks run in parallel, so there's always something on.
Daytime — workshops Hands-on sessions of one to two hours, 10 to 40 participants. They run alongside the talks and need separate sign-up.
All day — badge-hacking area Tables with soldering tools where you can modify, reflash, and extend the FAETCon badge.
All day — demo area and booths Attendee projects plus booths from manufacturers, robotics teams, and FAET/KAI student startups.
Finale — badge hack demos The best badge hacks shown on stage, followed by the awards.
Evening — afterparty Informal hangout to close out the conference.
