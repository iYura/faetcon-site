Badge

Badge-hacking conferences like Hackaday Supercon give attendees an electronic badge that's meant to be programmed, soldered, and modified on the spot — not just worn. FAETCon is building its own badge, a natural fit for a faculty of electronics and telecommunications.

The FAETCon 2026 badge design and specs are in progress. Board photos, schematics, and firmware (open source) will appear here.

What we're planning

  • A PCB designed in-house by FAET students and faculty.
  • Open schematics and firmware on GitHub — hackable after the conference too.
  • An on-site badge-hacking area with soldering tools.
  • A small contest for the best badge hack.

Want to help design the badge or run a soldering workshop? Reach out via Contacts or Get involved.
